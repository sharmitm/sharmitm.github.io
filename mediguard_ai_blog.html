MediGuard AI — A Multi-Agent Healthcare Fraud Detection System

A technical overview of a Google ADK + Gemini-powered platform for identity, billing, and discharge-risk detection.

Healthcare fraud, billing errors, and discharge delays cost hospitals and insurers billions each year. MediGuard AI is a multi-agent, AI-powered system designed to analyze patient claims data and automatically detect identity misuse, billing anomalies, and operational blockers using Google’s Agent Development Kit (ADK) and Gemini models.

What MediGuard AI Does

MediGuard AI processes synthetic patient and claims data and runs it through a 3-agent sequential pipeline:

  • Identity & Claims Fraud Detection
  • Billing Fraud Analysis
  • Discharge Blocker Assessment

The system outputs a unified fraud and discharge readiness report, providing hospital-grade decision support.

System Architecture

Data Sources

The system loads synthetic healthcare data in Synthea format:

  • patients.csv — Demographics
  • claims.csv — Claim summaries
  • claim_lines.csv — Itemized billing
  • encounters.csv — Hospital visits
  • procedures.csv — Completed and pending procedures

These are stored as Pandas DataFrames for fast, in-memory querying.

The Three AI Agents

MediGuard AI uses a sequential multi-agent pipeline, where each agent’s output feeds into the next.

Agent 1 — Identity & Claims Fraud Detection

Focus: Identity misuse and diagnosis/procedure inconsistencies.

  • Duplicate or mismatched SSN, DOB, name, or address
  • Conflicting demographic records across claims
  • Diagnosis–procedure mismatches
  • Unrealistic or abnormal claim amounts

Output: fraud_risk_score (0–100), identity_misuse_flag, reasons[]

Agent 2 — Billing Fraud Analysis

Focus: Billing patterns and cost anomalies.

  • Duplicate procedures
  • Add-on or unbundled billing
  • Cost outliers (high/low)
  • Diagnosis–procedure mismatches (re-validated)

Output: billing_risk_score (0–100), billing_flags[], billing_explanation

Agent 3 — Discharge Blockers Assessment

Focus: Operational readiness and fraud-dependent blocking.

  • Active inpatient encounters
  • Pending labs, imaging, or consults
  • Delays caused by fraud investigations

Output: discharge_ready (boolean), blockers[], delay_hours

Key Tools Available to Agents

  • fetch_patient_data_tool — retrieves patient records
  • calculate_claim_statistics — computes averages, outliers, ranges
  • check_patient_consistency — validates demographic alignment
  • analyze_diagnosis_procedure_match — checks coding correctness
  • get_active_encounters — identifies current hospitalizations
  • get_pending_procedures — lists incomplete clinical tasks
  • check_discharge_readiness — determines discharge blockers
